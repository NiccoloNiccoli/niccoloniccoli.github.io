Latent diffusion autoencoders are used as the basis for a practical video compressor. The method encodes sparse keyframes in latent space, entropy-codes those latents, and reconstructs intermediate frames through diffusion-based temporal interpolation, outperforming H.265/HEVC across tested bitrates.
